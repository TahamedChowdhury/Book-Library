Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Literary Haven

What are Literary Haven's hours of operation?

+

Our store is open Monday through Friday from 9am to 8pm, Saturday from 10am to 7pm, and Sunday from 11am to 5pm. We're closed on major holidays, but always announce special hours on our social media channels and website.

Do you have parking available?

+

Yes, we offer free customer parking in the lot behind our building. There's also street parking available, and we're just a short walk from the Literary District public parking garage.

Can you order a book that's not in your inventory?

+

Absolutely! We're happy to special order any in-print book. Most orders arrive within 3-5 business days, and we'll contact you as soon as your book arrives. There's no additional fee for this service. For out-of-print or rare books, please speak with our Rare Books Specialist who can help track down specific titles.

Do you ship books internationally?

+

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates vary based on weight and destination. Please contact us for a shipping quote before placing an international order. Delivery typically takes 7-14 business days, depending on the destination country's customs processing.

How can I find out about upcoming events?

+

We announce all our events on our website's Events page, our monthly newsletter, and our social media channels. You can also sign up for event-specific notifications by joining our Events mailing list. Most of our events are free, but some special events may require ticket purchase or RSVP.

Can I host a book club or private event at Literary Haven?

+

Yes! We have a dedicated event space available for book clubs, reading groups, and private literary events. The space can accommodate up to 30 people and includes audiovisual equipment. Contact our Events Manager at events@literaryhaven.com to inquire about availability and rates.

What benefits do Literary Haven members receive?

+

Our members enjoy a 10% discount on all purchases, advance notice of new arrivals and events, quarterly members-only events, and a free birthday book (up to $20 value). Membership costs $50 annually, and all proceeds support our community literacy programs.

How do I become a member?

+

You can sign up for membership in-store or on our website. Simply fill out the membership form and pay the annual fee. Your membership card will be available immediately if you join in-store, or we'll mail it to you within 5 business days if you join online.

Do you buy used books?

+

We selectively purchase used books in excellent condition. We're particularly interested in literary fiction, regional history, collectible classics, and specialized non-fiction. Please bring your books during our buying hours (Tuesday-Thursday, 11am-4pm) or email us photos and a list of titles you'd like to sell.

Are gift cards available?

+

Yes, gift cards are available in any amount from $10 to $500. They can be purchased in-store or online and never expire. Digital gift cards can be emailed directly to the recipient with a personalized message.
